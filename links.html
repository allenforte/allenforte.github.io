  • The Sonia Chalon Collection (1917-1977), at the University of North Texas Library. The collection consists of correspondence and memorabilia of Sonia Chalon, mezzo-soprano, First prize at the Concours Comoedia in Paris, France, 1925, Madeleine Forte's aunt. Includes letters and postcards between Sonia Chalon and Madeleine Forte and letters and cards from Camille Saint-Saens and Reynaldo Hahn.

  • THE ALLEN AND MADELEINE FORTE MEMORIAL COLLECTION at UNT
    Madeleine and Allen Forte donated their papers, books, scores, photos, recordings, and memorabilia to the University of North Texas Library, Denton, Texas.
